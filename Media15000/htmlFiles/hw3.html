Original Image

Clarity specifically targets the midtones and specific regions/portion sof the image rather than the entire image.
Vibrance pertains to the "intensity" on a portion of an image. A green image with vibrance -100 looks similar to a black and white image, but in actuality it changes the brightness.
Saturation is similar to vibrance except for the fact that it globally affects the image. That just means all the saturated colors in the image will be affected.
Shadows: Shadows works as an extra layer where you click the layer style icon which is the fx button on the bottom of the layers window. Shadows is an effect where duplicate images are repeated behind the original one, creating an animorphs book cover like effect. Sliders: They are very eye catching "backgrounds" that you can add information or text on.
